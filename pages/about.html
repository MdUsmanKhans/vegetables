Our Story

We started with one bad onion.

After one too many trips to the supermarket for vegetables that tasted like the plastic they came in, we decided to find a better way.

How it started

From a WhatsApp group to a full supply chain

In 2021, two cousins — one in Karachi, one in a village near Okara — started sending vegetables via courier. Friends wanted in. Then their friends. Before long, we had 200 households waiting every week.

We built SabziMandi to scale that trust. We visit every farm we partner with. We train our riders on how to handle produce. We test the soil. And we never, ever put a vegetable in cold storage if we can help it.

Our promise is simple: if it wasn't worth picking this morning, it doesn't go in your bag.

What drives us

The values behind every delivery

🌱

Radical freshness

We have a six-hour rule. If a vegetable can't reach your kitchen within six hours of harvest, it doesn't leave the farm under our name.

🤝

Farmer first

We pay our farmers above market rate — always. They take more care when they know they're being treated fairly. You taste the difference.

♻️

Zero waste packaging

All our packaging is reusable cloth or recycled paper. We collect the cloth bags back on the next delivery and wash them ourselves.

📍

Full traceability

Every item in your order tells you which farm it came from, when it was picked, and who picked it. Food with a face.

🧪

No chemicals

We work only with farms that use natural pest management. Our team visits unannounced to check. No chemicals, period.

💬

Honest pricing

Our prices fluctuate with the seasons. We don't hide this — we explain it. You see exactly what you're paying for and why.
